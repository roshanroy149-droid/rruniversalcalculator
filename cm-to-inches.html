Quick reference

Common centimeter-to-inch conversions.

CentimetersInches

How do I convert cm to inches?

Divide the centimeter value by 2.54: inches = cm ÷ 2.54. 1 inch is defined as exactly 2.54 cm by international agreement, so this conversion is exact, not an approximation.

How do I convert inches to cm?

Multiply the inch value by 2.54: cm = inches × 2.54. For example, 10 inches × 2.54 = 25.4 cm.

Where does 2.54 come from?

The international inch was formally standardized as exactly 2.54 cm in 1959 by agreement between the US, UK, and other Commonwealth countries, replacing several slightly different national definitions that existed before then.
