About RR Universal Calculator

What this is, and how the numbers work.

RR Universal Calculator is a set of everyday calculators built to answer one question at a time — no account, no app download, no data stored anywhere but your own browser tab.

How is tip calculated?

Multiply the bill by the tip percentage, then divide the total by the number of people. RR Universal Calculator does this live as you type.

Is the BMI number exact?

BMI is a screening estimate based on height and weight only — it doesn't account for muscle mass, age, or frame size, so treat the category as a starting point, not a diagnosis.

How is the loan payment formula calculated?

RR Universal Calculator uses the standard fixed-rate amortization formula, converting your annual rate to a monthly rate and solving for a level payment across the full term.

Is my password generator output stored anywhere?

No. Passwords are generated using your browser's built-in random number generator and never leave the page.
